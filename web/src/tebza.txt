Born at Lesedi Clinic in Soweto was a man who believes that your future is in and on your own hands. 

MR.Young Joey was raised in Molapo and soon moved to the west region of Roodepoort. Where he started his rap career at the tender age of 12 years.

He had a selection of rappers and singers he indulged his heart with, from the likes of Drake, J.Cole, The Notorious B.I.G. , 2pac, MMG Group, and a lost of amazing artists.

He switched High Schools, from Princess High School, THS Langlaagte, and West Ridge High School. All with the hopes that the situation would help him evolve and expand as a artist.

With all the academics and rap he stored time to work with 3 of the biggest artists in the art of South African Rap – Costa Titch, Nasty C and PatrickxxxLee – PatrickxxxLee was the person who motivated him through his early career. 

In all that he met the person who would be the motivation and reason for his debut album, Story of Nolkey, which is his girlfriend. Whos strives to help him become the man he wants to become.

The biggest blow he got through his time as a rapper is losing his biggest chance of being a big time artist was the challenge led by Stimerol on the 7th of March. 

*_"Disappointed and heartbreak is what I gained but my heart didn't waver from the person I'm going to become"_*, those were his words and one of the bounce back sentence of his career.
